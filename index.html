About Me

My name is Karla Carrera and I am an inspiring Software Developer located in the Seattle area. Ever since I can remember I have always been intrigued about the possibilities of software and its ability to connect us with new experiences. I was mainly interested in how things worked and why they worked the way they did, rather than the content presented on a screen.

My curiosity of technology led me to pursue a career in Software Development where I am currently learning the basics of web development and software programming. I will be graduating in March of 2025, hoping to land an internship at Meta or Microsoft to further my knowledge and understanding of technology.

I currently hold a leadership position of a lead cook in a local restaurant that has helped me develop time management, critical thinking, and problem solving skills. All of which I am able to use in the evolution of my software development career.
